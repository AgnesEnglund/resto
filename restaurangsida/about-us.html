Mazzarini is a great place to meet friends and grab something to eat and drink, just about any time of the day.

Come by our sunny bistro in the morning for fresh-baked pastries, cold-pressed juices and steamed coffees. Get your day started right.

Later in the day, drop by for our daily specials, salads and hot homemade meals. Experience our signature flatbreads, served with select wines and draft beers. By candlelight.

We are a family business, and everything is carefully made or selected by us. Mazzarini is a bright, lively place, in the heart of Stockholm. We hope you feel at home.
